How do I create an artist profile? +

Sign up with the "User" role, then go to your dashboard and click "Edit Profile". Fill in your stage name, category, bio, city, and add image URLs for your cover and profile photos.

How do I post an Open Call? +

Register as an "Owner", go to your dashboard, and click "Post New Open Call". Fill in the title, description, budget, and category. Your call will be live immediately.

Is LocalGems free to use? +

Yes. Creating a profile and browsing the platform is completely free. We believe talent should be accessible to everyone.

How do I apply to an Open Call? +

Browse the Open Calls page, find an opportunity that fits, and click "Apply Now". You'll need to be logged in as a User. Write a short message and submit your application.

How do I contact support? +

Email us at support@localgems.com and we'll get back to you within 24 hours.
